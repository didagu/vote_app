Ideation & Idea Management

Capture, enrich, connect, evaluate and discuss ideas and create concepts

Use crowd ideation to bring opportunities alive

Identify and create new products, services and business models. Activate and engage the crowd. Now!

If it's improving your business or creating new products, services and business models for growth: Unleashing the creative potential of employees, clients and partners is a key to success. The challenges in creating and maintaining a world class ideation and idea management are

  • Creating a collaborative ideation process
  • Selection and implementation of engagement models
  • Community activating and keeping the communities alive
  • Choosing idea and concept assessment methods and criteria
  • Connecting idea challenges to business opportunities
  • Creating the process from ideas to product and solution portfolios and roadmaps
  • KPIs, innovation governance and controlling

We at ITONICS have developed an integrated framework including process, methodology, software support and best practices in various industries to help clients establish a successful idea management function within the front end of innovation. We help global innovation leaders in developing future-driven and sustainable growth strategies.
